What must I do to survive?

  1. You have to do everything you can to keep your ship from sinking before you get to Japan.
  2. You should try to find something to eat as soon as possible.
  3. You should help the sick men as best you can.
  4. It would be a good idea to keep updating the record of your voyage in your rutter.
  5. You must keep your demoralized crew in order and continue to assert your authority.
  6. You must pilot the ship into a safe harbor in Japan.

How do I steer the ship?

  1. To change direction, turn the wheel to PORT (left) or STARBOARD (right).
  2. Compass directions won't work, you're in the middle of a storm!
  3. If you turn the wheel to PORT or STARBOARD, the ship will continue turning in that direction and you will flounder.
  4. STRAIGHTEN THE WHEEL once the ship has turned, to maintain course and to avoid capsizing.

I'm tired!

  1. You're tired and hungry.
  2. Better not lay down on your bunk, though. You'll fall asleep.
  3. Maybe you could find something to eat.

I'm hungry!

  1. An experienced seaman like yourself would probably keep a secret stash of emergency food somewhere.
  2. Perhaps in your cabin?
  3. Check your desk.
  4. That's right, they're gone. Someone must have taken them!
  5. Didn't you just see Jan Roper come out of your cabin?
  6. Roper took your apples! Why don't you go into the Mate's Cabin and get them?

How can I get the crew on watch?

  1. Go to the crew's quarters, Below Decks.
  2. LOOK AT THE CREWMEN to decide who's fit to take a watch.
  3. Send those men out on watch.
  4. Vinck, Ginsel and Maetsukker are strong enough to go out.
  5. Show some authority if any of them balk!

What about my rutters?

  1. They're valuable, so you keep them in your sea chest.
  2. You'll need a key to unlock it. Have you seen one around?
  3. The key is in your desk, which also holds the quill pen you'll need to write in it.

How can I help Spillbergen?

  1. He keeps asking for water, doesn't he?
  2. Try looking for water.
  3. There's a flagon of water hidden in the cabin.
  4. Did you search his desk?
  5. There's a secret compartment in the desk. The flagon is inside.

What about my pet rat?

  1. Oh, do you mean Gerald?
  2. You don't have a pet rat named Gerald!
  3. It's a joke! Now, stop reading the hints and play the game!

How do I get through the reef?

  1. Sail due west after straightening the wheel.
  2. When the reef is sighted, turn to Port to avoid hitting it.
  3. Soon after, you will see a gap to Starboard, so turn towards it.
  4. You'll be aimed very close to the gap, but you've lost some speed, so turn to port to get a boost from the wind.
  5. Once you've done that, turn to Starboard to enter the gap.

We've lost our foresails!

  1. Is the crew on deck?
  2. If not, get them! This is an emergency!
  3. Try ringing the bell. That ought to wake them up.
  4. Now that they're on deck, order them to repair the foresails.

I can't hold onto the wheel!

  1. You're too weak to fight the waves and current.
  2. Maybe you could use some help.
  3. Is anyone nearby?
  4. Vinck should be nearby. Ask him to help you.

I'm hungry!

  1. You won't live very long without getting something to eat.
  2. So eat the food that's on the tray!
  3. After you do that, a woman will come in.
  4. Indicate to her that you're still hungry.
  5. POINT TO THE TRAY or POINT TO MY STOMACH will suffice.
  6. She'll bring you more food and your clothes!
  7. Eat the second tray of food and you'll survive!

I was clumsy and I'm sorry!

  1. Tell the woman that. She'll appreciate it.
  2. Just do your best to apologize.

How can I fight off these henchmen?

  1. You can't beat them, but don't worry, you won't be killed.
  2. At least not if you fight back.

How can I avoid being killed by Omi?

  1. Be polite.
  2. Answer his questions!
  3. Address your answers to the priest. He's translating.
  4. You should know the answers already.
  5. Question 1: I AM ENGLISH
  6. Question 2: I AM FROM HOLLAND
  7. Question 3: I'M THE LEADER
  8. Once you've answered the questions and Omi kills the henchman, get out of there!

Where are my rutters?

  1. Have you looked on your ship?
  2. It's at anchor in the harbor!
  3. There's a rowboat at the waterfront.
  4. Just row out and climb aboard.
  5. You're the pilot. Just go aft towards your quarters.
  6. Nope, the samurai guards won't let you in. Maybe some other time.

Yabu

What do I say to Yabu?

  1. Answer the questions he asks. Some are the same as Omi's.
  2. It's also very important to notice how Yabu and Father Sebastio are getting along with each other.
  3. Question 1: I'M JOHN BLACKTHORNE.
  4. Question 2: I SAILED FROM HOLLAND.
  5. Question 3: I'M THE PILOT.
  6. Question 4: I SAILED THROUGH THE STRAIT OF MAGELLAN.

Yabu orders me killed!

  1. You need to show him there is a reason to keep you alive.
  2. You can't tell him anything, because you don't speak Japanese and Sebastio would lie about anything really useful.
  3. Demonstrate that you are not the same sort of 'barbarian' as Sebastio.
  4. Sebastio is a Catholic, you are a Protestant. In the year 1600, this is significant.
  5. In the year 1600, crosses would be an abomination to a good English Protestant.
  6. When Sebastio's crucifix dangles near you, grab it...
  7. Then break it or throw it on the ground.
  8. Then remember that courtesy is very important in this culture.
  9. Better bow to Yabu to show you didn't mean him any insult by that action.

How can I get Rodrigues' help?

  1. He asks you specific questions any English Pilot would know the answers to.
  2. If you study the map that came in your game package, you will find the answers.
  3. If you don't have a map, perhaps you really ARE a pirate!

What can Rodrigues do for me?

  1. He's obviously a respected person, maybe he has privileges you don't.
  2. Perhaps he can get you into your ship to search for your rutters.
  3. First, you should engage his interest. Talk to him about your ship.
  4. Then just follow his lead!

What about the archers?

  1. If you don't watch out, they'll think you're trying to escape, and shoot you!
  2. You could explain yourself...if you could speak Japanese.
  3. Rodrigues could explain, but he can't watch the archers while he's rowing.
  4. That's why he asked you to!
  5. Watch the archers, and when they act hostile, tell Rodrigues!

We're back on the Erasmus.

  1. This may be your ship, but the samurai are in charge now.
  2. This is Rodrigues' show. Just follow his lead, and let him do the talking.

How do I defeat the attacking guards?

  1. Find your pistol.
  2. Then find your powder horn.
  3. ...and blow them away!
  4. Of course, they aren't attacking, there is no pistol, and no powder horn.
  5. Hint-browsing is considered barbaric in Japan.

Why does Toranaga have me killed?

  1. Courtesy, respect, politeness. You are lacking in these.
  2. You could emulate Hiro-matsu, and kneel as he did.
  3. Or you could show some courage. Bow and then sit as an equal would!
  4. You can look at Toranaga, which is disrespectful: "A cat can look at a king." Beyond that, you are in grave danger.

What should I do with Alvito?

  1. Choose a language in which to speak to Alvito.
  2. Tell Toranaga that Alvito is your enemy.
  3. And remember that while he is your enemy, he is also not a fool.

Why should I obey Alvito?

  1. Because you were asked to.
  2. Because you are in the way if a fight starts!
  3. Toranaga and Ishido hate each other, so you don't want to be between them.

How do I prevent Ishido from killing Toranaga?

  1. You could try punching him out.
  2. Or kicking him.
  3. Or do nothing, since he isn't going to kill Toranaga.
  4. Not now, at any rate.

  2. He's a Dominican friar. All the priests you've met so far are Portuguese Jesuits.
  3. The Portuguese Jesuits threw him in prison. He hates the Portuguese.

  2. Ask him about things.
  3. For example, ask him about Toranaga or Japan or the Jesuits.
  4. Ask him to teach you Japanese. He knows a lot.

  3. Get in Mariko's litter.

What should I do about Yabu?

  1. First, notice that he's nervous.
  2. Ask him about the 'plan.'
  3. Maybe he's up to something!
  4. Perhaps you should follow him and find out.
  5. Discretion, discretion! Don't follow too close.

How can I survive the ninja attack?

  1. You could try fighting them off.
  2. Well, first you will need some weapons.
  3. Your swords and powder horn are in your quarters.
  4. The ninja haven't much experience with firearms, so your pistol will help a lot.
  5. They will slow down, confused and scared, each time you fire the pistol.
  6. But your pistol takes time to reload, so you're going to have to retreat and hope for help.
  7. And each time you use it, they get less scared of it.

They've taken Mariko!

  1. You can usually avoid this by telling Mariko to follow you.
  2. If they have her, firing the pistol will give her a chance to escape.

I can't get away from the ninja!

  1. You'll have to block them and slow them down.
  2. Firing the pistol at them slows them down.
  3. Closing and barring doors in their way slows them down.
  4. The iron-bound door between the Audience Chamber and the Inner Corridor can be closed and barred.
  5. You can also slow them down by shooting at them through the crack they make in that door.
  6. But you'll be better off finding a safer place to retreat to.
  7. Have you been back to the Private Quarters?
  8. When you go back, Kiritsubo will show you the way into a secret room.
  9. Once you're in there with the women, you should close the bolts!

How can I escape the explosion?

  1. You can go out onto the balcony.
  2. First you have to open the catches on the balcony door.
  3. They're rusty, so hit them with something.
  4. ...the pistol, for example.

How can I prevent Mariko's death?

  1. Get her into the Secret Redoubt.
  2. Then convince her to go onto the Balcony.
  3. But she won't go, will she?
  4. She thinks her death is inevitable.
  5. So force her!
  6. But if you do that the ninja will capture her.
  7. She's right. Your love is doomed, and if she's captured by Ishido, the hostages will never be freed.
  8. It's tragic, but there is no way out for her.
  9. Honor her faith, and bless her after she dies.

  1. It gives you something to read.
  2. It also reminds you that you can get a new ship.
  3. You should tell Toranaga that.

What does Toranaga want?

  1. He wants you to tell him what you know.
  2. What did you find out during the attack on the castle?
  3. What did Yabu do before the attack?
  4. Did you try to follow him?
  5. You ought to try that!
  6. Tell Toranaga that Yabu betrayed him.
  7. >TORANAGA, YABU BETRAYED YOU
  8. Tell Toranaga how Yabu betrayed him.
  9. >TORANAGA, YABU LET IN THE NINJA
  10. Tell Toranaga what Yabu said to the ninja.
  11. >TORANAGA, I AM KASIGI YABU

Read me first.

  1. This section tells you how the points in each scene are scored. You get five points for each correct action listed. It is possible to finish some scenes without getting all the points.
    Think twice before reading further! Don't spoil the game for yourself!

Erasmus (11 actions)

  1. Straightening the wheel.
  2. Getting your apple back from Roper.
  3. Eating the apple.
  4. Finding the key to the sea chest.
  5. Writing in your rutter.
  6. Finding the flagon in the Captain's desk.
  7. Giving the Captain water.
  8. Sending Vinck, Ginsel, and Maetsukker out on watch.
  9. Ringing the bell to get the crew on deck.
  10. Fixing the foresails.
  11. Getting through the reef into the harbor.

Anjiro (4 actions)

  1. Eating both trays of food.
  2. Fighting back against Sebastio's henchmen.
  3. Getting through Omi's interrogation.
  4. Trying to get your rutters back.

Yabu (2 actions)

  1. Breaking Sebastio's crucifix.
  2. Telling Yabu about your voyage.

Pit (5 actions)

  1. Saving Vinck if he's the one picked to die, or saving yourself if you are picked.
  2. Fighting the samurai, taking one as a hostage.
  3. Agreeing to behave.
  4. Lying down.
  5. Taking a bath.

Rodrigues (4 actions)

  1. Answering Rodrigues about where you trained.
  2. Answering Rodrigues about the Lizard.
  3. Telling Rodrigues about the Erasmus.
  4. Warning Rodrigues about the archers.

Voyage to Osaka (11 actions)

  1. Grabbing the gunwale.
  2. Restoring order on the oar deck.
  3. Getting the oarsmen to row again.
  4. Throwing oar to Rodrigues.
  5. Dropping anchor once in the bay.
  6. Getting into the skiff.
  7. Finding Rodrigues.
  8. Making a rope of kimonos.
  9. Adding loincloths to rope.
  10. Finding the ledge.
  11. Getting Yabu's attention.

Toranaga (3 actions)

  1. Defying convention by bowing but not kneeling.
  2. Moving to the dais when Ishido arrives.
  3. Telling Toranaga Alvito is your enemy.

Prison (6 actions)

  1. Defeating the thug.
  2. Speaking Spanish to Domingo.
  3. Asking Domingo to teach you Japanese.
  4. Joining the line of prisoners.
  5. Avoiding the bandit's attack.
  6. Getting in Yabu's palanquin.

Mariko (3 actions)

  1. Greeting or bowing to Mariko or Toranaga.
  2. Smiling or winking at Yaemon.
  3. Asking Mariko about the prophecy.

Escape (13 actions)

  1. Seeing Toranaga in the hut.
  2. Seeing Toranaga and Kiritsubo switch.
  3. Grabbing the parchment from Ishido.
  4. Giving the parchment to 'Kiri.'
  5. Distracting Ishido by acting nuts.
  6. Reaching the woods successfully.
  7. Saving Mariko during ambush.
  8. Finding the ronin.
  9. Slowing down the messenger so Buntaro can kill him.
  10. Helping Mariko when she 'faints.'
  11. Remembering the word for 'water.'
  12. Killing the Gray leader.
  13. Killing the second Gray.

Earthquake (4 actions)

  1. Saving Toranaga.
  2. Saving Mariko.
  3. Pissing in the fissure.
  4. Giving swords to Toranaga after his are lost.

Journey to Yedo (3 actions)

  1. Bathing with Mariko.
  2. Making love to Mariko.
  3. Getting back to your room safely.

Ochiba (3 actions)

  1. Checking your swords.
  2. Giving the camellia to Ochiba.
  3. Telling Ishido you are not a barbarian.

Departure (1 action)

  1. Killing Yamazaki.

Seppuku (1 action)

  1. Carrying Mariko away.

Ninja (6 actions)

  1. Asking Yabu about the plan.
  2. Hiding from the ninja in the cellars.
  3. Seeing Yabu in the cellars let in the ninja.
  4. Shooting the ninja leader.
  5. Telling Mariko to follow you.
  6. Blessing Mariko.

Yokohama (1 action)

  1. Picking up Vinck.

Aftermath (3 actions)

  1. Reading the sealed scroll.
  2. Reporting that you saw Yabu.
  3. Reporting the password.
